About Me

Salutations! My name is John Randolph and I am an aspiring web developer. I have come here with little experience or knowledge in this industry. I am learning everything from scratch. This is undoubtedly the most intellectually strenuous exercise I have ever voluntarily participated in. Even with a Bachelors Degree from Rutgers, I have found myself stuck in careers in the service industry. I realized if I ever wanted to recareer I needed to learn new skills and push myself out of my comfort zone. Coding is the lifeblood of modern technological society. What I learn here can be transmitted to diverse fields of industry the world over. I am grateful for the opportunity to change the direction of my professional life!
